About me.

Richard Howe

GitHub

My name is Richard James Howe, I go by many aliases (two; howerj, work) and I am an electrical engineer by trade having just graduated. I have passions for reading, programming, watching silly cartoons with my engaged-to-be-engaged girlfriend Leonor and tinkering with electrical equipment.

My languages of choice include C, Forth and VHDL with a spattering of various scripting languages to keep it all together, if it is not written in one of these languages, it is not worth it...until I learn the new ones. I enjoy more of the low level programming and concepts in computing and wish to gain as much knowledge about these systems from the transistor upwards.

I believe in using open source licenses where possible and as such have released all my personal code for other people to use, although I am not too fussed as to what license it is under (usually the LGPL).
